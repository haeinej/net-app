Why This Word

Ohm is a unit of electrical resistance — the measure of how much a material resists or conducts a signal passing through it. On this platform, the whole system is about reducing resistance between two people's thinking. The recommendation engine literally measures resonance.


But nobody thinks of that first. First they think: the sound. The vibration. The thing you say when you're centering yourself. Three letters that feel like a deep breath. It's calm. It's grounded. It doesn't explain anything — it just sits there, humming, until someone asks what it means. And then you get to explain resonance, and the conversation becomes the product.
