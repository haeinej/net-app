Algorithm rules for ohm.

- Optimize for resonance, not topic similarity.
- Replies are the strongest behavioral signal.
- Feed composition is retrieval over stored embeddings, not an LLM call at refresh time.
- No popularity snowballing.
- No exposed metrics.
- Crossings are private.
- Shifts can surface publicly without exposing the thread.
- Interests may exist internally for cold start, but never as a visible taxonomy.
- The acceptance gate is non-negotiable.
