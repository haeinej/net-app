Onboarding flow.

Step 1: Identity

- name
- photo
- cohort year
- city
- concentration
- auth fields

Step 2: Internal cold-start prompts

- collect up to three freeform lines about what is alive in the user's thinking
- never call these skills
- keep them internal
- do not render them on the visible profile surface

Step 3: First thought

- require the first posted thought before entering the app
- this becomes the user's primary visible signal

- the spec contains mixed language about interests
- the app keeps them as internal onboarding-only cold-start data
- the visible profile surface no longer shows them
