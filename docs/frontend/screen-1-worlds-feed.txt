Screen 1: Worlds

Purpose:

- discovery
- notification intake
- shifts in public view

Rules:

- Feed is a stack of thought cards on warm ground
- Notification center is inline, never a separate screen
- The header has exactly three elements: notification circle, logo, compose
- No counts, no urgency badges, no "trending"

Notification behavior:

- Circle only appears when pending replies exist
- Tap reveals the panel inline from the top
- Rows show replier, preview, and the thought they replied to
- Actions are Accept and Ignore
- Accept creates a conversation
- Ignore is silent

Content behavior:

- Thought cards dominate the feed
- Shifts may appear in the feed
- Crossings never appear in the feed
