Testing checklist for the updated spec.

Seed:

- at least 3 users with distinct concentrations
- at least 10 thoughts across different tones and abstraction levels
- at least 2 pending replies
- at least 1 accepted conversation
- at least 1 conversation with 10 or more exchanges

Verify:

- pending replies appear only in Worlds notifications
- accept creates a conversation and preserves the original reply
- ignore removes the pending reply silently
- reply length validation enforces 50 to 300 characters
- crossings do not appear in Worlds
- shifts may appear in Worlds
- visible profiles do not render internal interests
