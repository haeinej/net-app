Core statements:

- Where your thoughts find someone.
- Post a thought. Someone replies. You accept. You talk.
- Three screens only: Worlds, Conversations, Me.
- No follow, like, react, metrics, or popularity engine.
- Replies are written text and require acceptance before they become visible in a conversation.
- Crossings are private profile artifacts.
- Shifts can appear in Worlds, but the underlying conversation stays private.

Current implementation interpretation:

- Interests remain an internal cold-start signal.
- Interests are no longer shown on the visible profile surface.
- Resonance signatures replace the older "underlying question" language.
- The existing `question_embedding` column is used as the primary resonance embedding until a fuller schema migration lands.
